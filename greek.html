Greek Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Feta Cheese
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
  2. Cut the cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, and green bell peppers into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Crumble the feta cheese and slice the Kalamata olives.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken into strips.
  5. In a large bowl, combine all the vegetables, chicken, feta cheese, and olives.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together red wine vinegar, extra virgin olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat all the ingredients.

Notes

  • You can add some fresh lemon juice for extra flavor.
  • Feel free to customize your salad with other Greek salad ingredients like Tzatziki sauce or garlic cloves.
  • Serve with warm pita bread for a complete meal.

Enjoy your Greek Chicken Salad
