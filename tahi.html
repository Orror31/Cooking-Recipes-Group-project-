Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • Green Curry Paste
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken or Tofu (your choice)
  • Eggplant
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Thai Basil Leaves
  • Green Beans
  • Fish Sauce

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat the coconut milk and green curry paste.
  2. Add chicken or tofu and cook until it's done.
  3. Add eggplant, bamboo shoots, and green beans.
  4. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with fish sauce and Thai basil leaves.

Notes

  • You can adjust the spiciness by adding more or less green curry paste.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables or proteins of your choice.
  • Serve with steamed jasmine rice for a complete meal.

Enjoy your Thai Green Curry!
