Mozrella Pizza

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Yeast and Water
  • Salt and Suger/honey
  • Tomato/ketchup
  • Cheese
  • meat
  • olive
  • sea food

Instructions

  1. preheat the oven 350.F
  2. In a large bowl, water,purpose Flour and yest
  3. let it rise for a while
  4. put whatever you like of your choice
  5. put your own choice of toppings

Notes

  • putpose flour is the best to use
  • don't use expired Yeast
  • you can put any amount of sweetness you like but a tablespoon is the best
  • you can add anything you like to make it your own

Enjoy your pizza
